# 한국어 배우기 · Learn Korean

A step-by-step Korean learning site built for **Filipino (Tagalog) and English speakers** — from zero to real conversations.

## Features

- **4 languages per line** — Korean · Romanization · English · Filipino
- **Romanization toggle** — switch between hyphenated (An-nyeong) and plain (Annyeong)
- **Filipino learner tips** — each lesson highlights connections to Tagalog grammar and vocabulary
- **Interactive quiz** with 4 modes and 8 word categories
- **No login, no install** — works offline after first load, just open the HTML files

## Lessons Overview

### Level 1 · 한글 Hangul
Learn the Korean alphabet — 19 consonants, 21 vowels, syllable block structure, stroke order rules, and linking sounds (연음). Click any character card for pronunciation tips.

### Level 2 · 생존 한국어 Survival Phrases
35 essential phrases across 5 situations: greetings, basics, shopping, directions, and emergencies. Each phrase includes a word-by-word breakdown and formality level badge. Includes 3 full dialogue scenarios.

### Level 3 · 문법 Grammar Comparison
Side-by-side comparison of Korean, English, and Filipino grammar across 6 topics: word order (SOV vs SVO vs VSO), particles, verb tense and speech levels, negation, questions, and honorifics.

### Level 4 · 숫자 Numbers & Counters
Korean's two number systems (Sino-Korean 한자어 vs Native Korean 순우리말), 12 essential counters, the 만(10,000) unit system, telling time, dates, age, phone numbers, and prices in ₩.

### Level 5 · 대화 Real Conversations
Full dialogues across 6 real situations — immigration, currency exchange, subway, taxi, Korean BBQ, café, clothes shopping, traditional market bargaining, language exchange meeting, and pharmacy. Every line includes Korean, romanization, English, and Filipino, plus culture notes.
