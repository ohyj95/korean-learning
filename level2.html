At a Café
카페에서 · Ordering coffee
Staff
어서 오세요! 주문하시겠어요? Eo-seo o-se-yo! Ju-mun-ha-si-ge-sseo-yo? Welcome! Are you ready to order?
You
아메리카노 한 잔 주세요. A-me-ri-ka-no han jan ju-se-yo. One americano please.
Staff
따뜻하게요, 아이스요? Tta-tteu-ta-ge-yo, a-i-seu-yo? Hot or iced?
You
아이스로 주세요. 얼마예요? A-i-seu-ro ju-se-yo. Eol-ma-ye-yo? Iced please. How much?
Staff
사천오백원이에요. Sa-cheon-o-baek-won-i-e-yo. It's 4,500 won.

Asking for Directions
길 묻기 · Finding your way
You
저기요! 지하철역이 어디예요? Jeo-gi-yo! Ji-ha-cheol-yeok-i eo-di-ye-yo? Excuse me! Where is the subway station?
Stranger
저쪽으로 쭉 가세요. Jeo-jjok-eu-ro jjuk ga-se-yo. Go straight that way.
You
감사합니다! Gam-sa-ham-ni-da! Thank you!

Meeting Someone New
처음 만날 때 · First meeting
Korean
안녕하세요! 처음 뵙겠습니다. An-nyeong-ha-se-yo! Cheo-eum boep-get-seum-ni-da. Hello! Nice to meet you for the first time.
You
안녕하세요! 저는 [이름]이에요. An-nyeong-ha-se-yo! Jeo-neun [name]-i-e-yo. Hello! I'm [name].
Korean
어느 나라에서 왔어요? Eo-neu na-ra-e-seo wa-sseo-yo? What country are you from?
You
필리핀에서 왔어요. Pil-li-pin-e-seo wa-sseo-yo. I'm from the Philippines.
Korean
한국어 잘 하시네요! Han-guk-eo jal ha-si-ne-yo! Your Korean is great!
